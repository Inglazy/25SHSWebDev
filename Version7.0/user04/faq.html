Frequently Asked Questions

What time does the Gaming Club meet?
We meet every Friday from 3:30 PM to 5:30 PM in Room 101.
Do I need my own console or laptop?
Nope! We provide equipment, but you're welcome to bring your own.
Can beginners join?
Absolutely. Whether you’re a pro or just starting out, you’re welcome!
Is there a membership fee?
None at all! Gaming Club is completely free to join.
