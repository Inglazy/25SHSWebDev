About Trap Shooting

Trap shooting is a sport where people aim at clay targets launched into the air. It requires focus, precision, and control. There are different disciplines such as singles, doubles, and handicap shooting.

Singles

Shooters fire at one clay target launched from a single machine at a time.

Doubles

Two clay targets are launched at the same time for shooters to hit both targets quickly.

Handicap

Shooters stand farther back from the trap house, making it harder to hit the targets.
