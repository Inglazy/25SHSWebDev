Gear & Shooting Tips

Having the right gear and following good tips can help improve your trap shooting skills.

Essential Gear
  • Shotgun (12 gauge preferred)
  • 7.5 shot, 3 dram load. No Buckshot or Slugs. You are responsible for the shells you shoot. (ammunition)
  • Hearing and eye protection
  • Shooting vest or shell pouch
  • Trap shooting glasses
Helpful Tips
  • Keep your feet shoulder-width apart for balance.
  • Focus on the target, not the barrel.
  • Lead the target slightly depending on its speed and direction.
  • Practice consistency and follow through after each shot.
  • Stay relaxed and breathe evenly before shooting.
