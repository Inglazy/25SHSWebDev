Trap Shooting Team – Frequently Asked Questions

When are practices held?
We practice Tuesdays and Thursdays at 4:00 PM at the county range.
Can I join if I'm a beginner?
Yes! No experience is required. We welcome all skill levels.
Is gear provided?
We have team shotguns and safety equipment available for use.
How do I sign up?
You can sign up during the activity fair or speak with Coach Jensen.
Are there competitions?
Yes, we compete in meets with local schools and regional events.
