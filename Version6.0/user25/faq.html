Trap Shooting Team FAQ

When are trap shooting practices?
Practices are every Tuesday and Thursday at 4:00 PM.
Do I need my own shotgun?
No, we have a few team shotguns available to borrow.
Is prior experience required?
No experience needed! We train beginners from scratch.
Where are meets held?
Meets are held at the county range or hosted by other schools.
How do I join the team?
Contact the coach or visit our sign-up page under the Join Us tab.
