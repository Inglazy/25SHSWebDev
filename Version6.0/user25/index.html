What Is This Website About?

Trap shooting is one of the most exciting and challenging sports out there. On this website, you'll find everything you need to know if you're just getting started or if you're already part of a team. We’ll go over how trap shooting works, the kind of gear you need, tips to help you shoot better, and cool photos from real events. I’m a high school student who shoots trap, so this is made from my perspective. My goal is to share what I’ve learned and help others who might be interested in trying it out. Whether you’re aiming for fun or competition, this site is here to help you hit your target!
