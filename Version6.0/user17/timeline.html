Key Milestones

  • Michael Jacksons Era, 1960-2009: Michael Jackson, often referred to as the "King of Pop," revolutionized the music industry with his groundbreaking albums like Thriller and Bad, shaping pop culture for decades. His unmatched talent and innovative music videos made him a global icon, garnering countless awards and accolades. Tragically, Jackson's life was cut short when he passed away on June 25, 2009, leaving behind a legacy that continues to influence artists worldwide.
  • Tupac's Era, 1990's: Tupac Shakur, a highly influential rapper and actor, became known for his raw, socially conscious lyrics and his ability to capture the struggles of urban life. His albums, like All Eyez on Me and Me Against the World, catapulted him to superstardom, making him one of the most iconic figures in hip-hop history. Tragically, Tupac was fatally shot in a drive-by shooting on September 13, 1996, at just 25 years old, leaving behind a lasting impact on music and culture.
  • Kendrick Lamar's rise 2000-present: Kendrick Lamar's rise to fame began with his early mixtapes and the 2012 release of good kid, m.A.A.d city, which showcased his storytelling and lyrical prowess, earning him widespread recognition in the hip-hop world. His unique blend of introspective lyrics, social commentary, and experimental sound pushed the boundaries of hip-hop, culminating in multiple Grammy wins and the Pulitzer Prize for Music in 2018 for DAMN. Lamar's success continues to influence modern hip-hop, as he remains one of the genre's most respected and innovative artists.

  • The Weeknd's Popularity: The Weeknd, born Abel Tesfaye, rose to fame with his hauntingly smooth voice and atmospheric, genre-blending sound, which first gained attention through his mixtapes in 2010. His breakout album Starboy in 2016 and hits like "Blinding Lights" solidified him as a global pop icon, known for his fusion of R&B, pop, and electronic music. With numerous chart-topping singles and Grammy Awards, The Weeknd's success continues to redefine the landscape of contemporary music.
  • OsamaSon: OsamaSon, born Amari Deshawn Middleton on May 20, 2003, in Columbus, Ohio, is a rapper, songwriter, and producer from Goose Creek, South Carolina. He gained prominence in early 2023 with tracks like "Cts-V" and "Troops," leading to his debut album Osama Season in July 2023. His subsequent projects, including Flex Musix (December 2023) and Jump Out (January 2025), showcased his evolving sound, characterized by heavy bass and experimental production, often described as "maximalist rage." Despite challenges like early leaks, OsamaSon's music has resonated within the underground rap scene, earning critical acclaim and a growing fanbase..
