Meet the Teams

Learn about Minnesota's greatest sports franchises

🏀 Minnesota Timberwolves

The Timberwolves are Minnesota’s NBA team known for rising stars like Anthony Edwards. They've been making deep playoff pushes in recent years and are known for their exciting defense and fast pace.

⚾ Minnesota Twins

The Twins are one of MLB’s historic teams, with multiple World Series titles. They play at Target Field and have a passionate fan base.

🏈 Minnesota Vikings

The Vikings are an iconic NFL team, famous for their “Skol” chant and history of legendary players. They play at U.S. Bank Stadium and are always a playoff contender.
