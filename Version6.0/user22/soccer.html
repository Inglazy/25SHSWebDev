My Soccer Journey

I started playing soccer in 2016 with a local club team, and in 2020 I joined SSA Shakopee. I was first placed on the C2 team, which isn't the best but also not the worst. After putting in the work, I made the C1 team in 2022, which is the highest level. I played with them for two years, and now I'm training and getting ready to make the varsity soccer team at my high school.
