Minnesota Ultimate is a not profit orginization that offers year-round playing opportunities for both experienced and first time youth players alike. At Minnesota Ultimate, our high school programming is dedicated to fostering an inclusive and vibrant community that strengthens the sport of youth ultimate frisbee in Minnesota. By providing equitable opportunities for all athletes, we honor each player’s identity and background, ensuring everyone feels valued and respected. Our programming addresses the diverse needs of young athletes while creating structured, high-quality experiences that encourage participation and growth for all.
