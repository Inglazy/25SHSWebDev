Lebron James (King James)

born December 30, 1984 is an American professional basketball player for the Los Angeles Lakers of the National Basketball Association (NBA). Nicknamed "King James", he is the NBA's all-time leading scorer and has won four NBA championships from 10 NBA Finals appearances, having made eight consecutive appearances between 2011 and 2018. He also won the inaugural NBA Cup in 2023 with the Lakers and has won three Olympic gold medals as a member of the U.S. national team.

LeBron James's professional basketball career officially began in 2003 when he was selected first overall by the Cleveland Cavaliers in the NBA Draft. He made his NBA debut on october 29, 2003. then, in 2010 he joined the miami heat for 4 years, he then went back to the caveliers. and finally, in 2018 lebron joined the los angeles lakers where he currently is playing right now. The 40-year-old admitted that he was undecided over his future in the NBA after the Lakers were knocked out in the first round of the playoffs by the Minnesota Timberwolves. The Lakers were defeated by an Anthony Edwards-led Timberwolves 4-1 despite coming up against James and Luka Doncic. in a recent interview of the lakers loss in the first round of the playoffs lebrons james stated "I don't know," James said on his future. "I don't have an answer to that. Something I'll sit down with my family, my wife and my support group and kind of just talk through it and see what happens. And just have a conversation with myself on how long I want to continue to play. I don't know the answer to that right now, to be honest. So we'll see."
