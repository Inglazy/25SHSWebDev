Why I Love Trap Shooting

Trap shooting is more than just a sport — it’s a way to sharpen your skills, stay focused, and have fun with friends. Whether I'm competing or just out at the range, there’s nothing like the sound of a clay breaking mid-air!

I usually shoot with a 12-gauge and love the feeling of improving my accuracy and reaction time. It’s a big part of what makes hunting even more fun too.
